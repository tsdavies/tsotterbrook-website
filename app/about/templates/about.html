About Me

Present Day

I am currently studying an MSc in Computing at IT Management at Cardiff University (2024/25), increasing my development skillset, generative AI usage and product/project management skills.

Career History

My journey into the technology industry began quite a long time ago, however, only in 2018 did I truly begin to find my way. Initially, in 2004, I was volunteering as in-game customer service and tester for a popular MMORPG, which sparked my interest in testing. In 2007, I was the director, administrator and tester for a small software house, with very big clients such as the European Space Agency. I took some time off in 2009 following the birth of my son and after a few awful jobs in call centres, I decided to return to education, gaining my BA in English Literature from Cardiff University in 2017. Following graduation, I joined Deloitte as an engagement support officer for large audits and the Agile Transformation team, which lead me to being trained, qualified and practicing as a Scrum Master...

My journey began in QA, where I honed my skills in ensuring the highest standards of product functionality and reliability. Working across diverse projects, I developed a proactive approach to quality assurance, identifying and resolving issues early in the development cycle to enhance product excellence and customer satisfaction. Transitioning into a Scrum Master role allowed me to broaden my impact, fostering collaboration, facilitating Agile practices, and empowering teams to achieve their best...

I am a strong advocate for Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion, I believe that fostering a supportive and inclusive culture is key to achieving outstanding results. I believe that my unique blend of technical expertise, Agile leadership, and quality assurance positions me to make a meaningful impact in any project or organisation.

Interests and Achievements

I love gaming in both tabletop and digital formats, as well as reading and writing fantasy. I have written six novels, although none are published yet. Recently, I had the privilege of being mentored by award-winning fantasy author Mark Lawrence and aim to be published in 2025.

Personal Life

I was born and raised in Toronto, Canada, and moved to Wales in 2007.
