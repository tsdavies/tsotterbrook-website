About Me

Personal Life

I was born and raised in Toronto, Canada, and moved to Wales after marrying my husband, Andy in 2007. We have two children, Shannon and James, and a Jack Russell Terrier named Falkor.

Career

My journey into the technology industry began quite a long time ago, however, only in 2018 did I truly begin to find my way. Initially, in 2004, I was volunteering as in game customer service and tester for a popular MMORPG, which sparked my interest in testing. In 2007, I was the director, administrator and tester for a small software house, with very big clients such as the European Space Agency. I took some time off in 2009 following the birth of my son and after a few awful jobs in call centres, I decided to return to education, gaining my BA in English Literature from Cardiff University in 2017. Following graduation, I joined Deloitte as an engagement support officer for large audits and the Agile Transformation team, which lead me to being trained, qualified and practicing as a Scrum Master. When the transformation pilot ended and I had to go back to being an administrator, it was too late for me. I wanted to be a Scrum Master, and the only way to ensure that was to get back into tech. In 2019, I landed a junior tester role, as it was clear the Scrum Master roles all required some knowledge of working in a development team. I worked as a tester for nearly four years and when a Scrum Master role came up at Creditsafe, where I was working as a test engineer, I was able to successfully secure the role and was soon blending a deep understanding of software quality with a passion for Agile leadership.

My journey began in QA, where I honed my skills in ensuring the highest standards of product functionality and reliability. Working across diverse projects, I developed a proactive approach to quality assurance, identifying and resolving issues early in the development cycle to enhance product excellence and customer satisfaction. Transitioning into a Scrum Master role allowed me to broaden my impact, fostering collaboration, facilitating Agile practices, and empowering teams to achieve their best. My background in QA gives me a unique perspective on the software development lifecycle, enabling me to guide teams in delivering high-value outcomes while maintaining a strong focus on quality. I am a strong advocate for Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion, I believe that fostering a supportive and inclusive culture is key to achieving outstanding results. I believe that my unique blend of technical expertise, Agile leadership, and quality assurance positions me to make a meaningful impact in any project or organisation.
